Tessaract

Tessaract is a revolutionary cryptocurrency for monetary transactions. Tessaract functions using the SHA256 hashing algorithm, the PGP encryption method for signing transactions, and blockchain verification technology.

This website was created using Flask, PostgreSQL, and Heroku.

Click the question mark in the top right corner to learn more about the creators.
This website is a simulation and does not actually have any monetary value attached to the coins.

Before adding coins to your balance you must be logged in.

Once logged in, navigate to your wallet and enter testaccount as the reciever, and enter your desired number of coins as the amount. Any crypto sent to testaccount will be added to your balance.

Once the current block is mined, the coins will be in your wallet.
